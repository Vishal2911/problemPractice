According to a recent survey, Biryani is the most ordered food. Raju wants to learn how to make 
world-class Biryani from a Kavita. Raju will be required to attend the Kavita's classes 
for X weeks, and the cost of classes per week is Y coins. What is the total amount of money that
Raju will have to pay? 

Input Format
The first line of input will contain an integer T â€” the number of test cases. The description of 
T test cases follows. 
The first and only line of each test case contains two space-separated integers X and Y, 
as described in the problem statement.

Output Format
For each test case, output on a new line the total amount of money that Raju will have to pay.

Sample 1:
Input                                                                   Output                                                                   
4                                                                        10
1 10                                                                     15   
1 15                                                                     20
2 10                                                                     30
2 15

Explanation:
Test case 1: Raju will be required to attend the Kavita's classes for 1 week and the cost 
of classes per week is 10 coins. Hence, Raju will have to pay 10 coins in total.
Test case 2: Raju will be required to attend the Kavita's classes for 1 week and the cost 
of classes per week is 15 coins. Hence, Raju will have to pay 15 coins in total. 
Test case 3: Raju will be required to attend the Kavita's classes for 2 weeks and the cost 
of classes per week is 10 coins. Hence, Raju will have to pay 20 coins in total.
Test case 4: Raju will be required to attend the Kavita's classes for 2 weeks and the cost 
of classes per week is 15 coins. Hence, Raju will have to pay 30 coins in total.
